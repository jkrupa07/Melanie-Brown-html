Aristotle believed that the ultimate goal of all human actions is to achieve a state he called eudaimonia, translated as human flourishing or living well. Achieving our well-being,
living well or even a positive work life balance can be a constant struggle. Fortunately, science can provide some guidance, with research revealing new ways to ageing well from nutritional supplements to cell regeneration, perhaps a cornerstone of human flourishing. Possibly ageing well, coupled with the most promising ways to delay ageing features at an intersection between cultivating a healthy mind and body, eating well and the benefits of exercise, other aspects of flourishing, and create the way to a better you.
