The Jalupro treatment experience
01. Before Your Treatment
Following your consultation, pre-care information will be discussed with you to ensure you receive the best possible outcome from your treatment.
When you attend your appointment, it is preferable you do not wear any make-up. If you are wearing make-up, it will be removed prior to your treatment. This will avoid any risk of infection.
Although your treatment will take 30-60 minutes, please allow additional time for your appointment as every patient is very important to me and treated on an individual basis. You are welcome to take a seat, help yourself to the selection of refreshments in the waiting room before and after your treatment if you would like to.
02. During Your Treatment
A topical numbing cream will be applied and removed after 20-40 minutes to allow it to take effect. The areas to be treated will be cleaned, make up removed if worn, and then disinfected. The numbing cream will reduce discomfort before administering multiple injections across the treatment area.
Immediately after treatment it is quite normal for there to be multiple raised bumps, slight redness and tenderness around the treated area. These signs will gradually disappear in a few days. The skin is once again cleansed and a soothing aftercare balm applied.
03. After Your Treatment
Please follow the aftercare advice to minimise adverse effects and maximise your treatment outcome:
  • Avoid washing your face or applying creams to the face for 12 hours following the treatment and wearing make up for 24 hours after treatment. 
  • Avoid direct exposure to ultraviolet rays, intense heat (very hot showers, saunas) or intense cold in the four weeks following the treatment as well as facial/massage treatments. 
  • For an optimal result, a treatment plan of 3-4 sessions at 2-3-week interval is recommended with lasting results up to 6 months.
  • You will be offered a review appointment at 2-3 weeks after your treatment to assess your progress.

What are amino acids?
Advanced skin rejuvenation treatments with hyaluronic acid and amino acids.
Fibroblasts are the major source of collagen production. Connective tissue is mainly made of collagen which gives tensile strength, density and support to the skin and structure to lift the face. Connective tissue integrity plays an important role in the ageing process. With time and lifestyle burden collagen is degraded. Young skin is rich in collagen and elastin, appears firm, smooth without wrinkles. Unfortunately, as we age mature skin loses collagen and elastin, thus lines and wrinkles appear.
